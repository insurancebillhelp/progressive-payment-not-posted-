Understanding “Payment Not Posted” or “Payment Not Showing”

A “payment not posted” issue occurs when a payment has been successfully submitted, but the billing system has not yet updated the account balance. This can create confusion, especially when the due date is near or the policy status still shows an outstanding balance.

  • Payment completed but still showing due
  • Balance not updated after payment
  • No confirmation or delayed update
  • Payment pending in account

In most cases, this is a temporary issue related to processing time rather than a failed payment.

Who This Issue Commonly Affects

  • Users who recently made an online payment
  • Guest payment users (without login)
  • Bank transfer (ACH) payments
  • Users paying close to the due date

Why Your Progressive Payment Is Not Posted or Not Showing

If your Progressive payment is not showing or not posted, it is usually related to payment processing time rather than a failed transaction. Different payment methods follow different processing timelines.

  • Payment processing delay – System update may take time
  • Bank clearance (ACH) – Can take 1–3 business days
  • Incorrect policy details – Payment applied incorrectly
  • Weekend or holiday delay – Processing may pause
  • System update lag – Backend delay in reflecting balance
